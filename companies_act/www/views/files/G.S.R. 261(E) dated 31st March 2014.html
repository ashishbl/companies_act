MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS

CORRIGENDA

New Delhi, the 31st March, 2014

G.S.R. 261(E).—In the notification of the Government of India in the Ministry of Corporate Affairs dated the 27th February, 2014 published in the Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, Section 3, Sub-section (i) vide G.S.R. No. 130(E), dated the 28th February, 2014 at page 2, line 20 for ‘‘promoting preventive health care’’ read ‘‘promoting health care including preventive health care’’.
