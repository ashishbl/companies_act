MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS
NOTIFICATION

New Delhi, the 31st March, 2015

S.O................ (E) In exercise of the powers conferred by section 458 of the Companies Act, 2013 (18 of 2013), the Central Government hereby delegates to the Regional Directors at Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Noida, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad and Shillong, the powers and functions vested in it under sub-section (5) of section 94 of the Companies Act, 2013, subject to the condition that the Central Government may revoke such delegation of powers or may itself exercise the powers under the said sub-section, if in its opinion such a course of action is necessary in the public interest.

2. This notification shall come into force with effect from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.
