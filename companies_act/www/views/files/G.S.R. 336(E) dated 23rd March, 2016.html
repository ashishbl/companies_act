MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS

NOTIFICATION

New Delhi, the 23rd March, 2016

G.S.R. 336(E).-In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 469 of the Companies Act, 2013 (18 of 2013), the Central Government hereby makes the following rules further to amend the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, namely:-

 

1.      (1) These rules may be called the Companies (Incorporation) Second Amendment Rules,  2016.

 

(2) They shall come into force on the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.

 

2.       In the Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014, in the Annexure, for Form No. INC-11,  shall be substituted.

 

 

[F.No. 01/13/2013 CL-V (Pt-I)]
