Subject:- Filling of MGT-10- clarification-regarding .

Sir,

In continuation of General Circular No. 06/2014 dated 29.03.2014 and 09/2014 dated 25.04.2014 , I am directed to inform you that stakeholders are required to fill Form MGT-10 physically, get it duly signed/certified by a professional and file it along with other required enclosures as attachments with the prescribed General E-Form No. GNL-2. This temporary arrangement will continue till an E-Form for MGT-10 is made available. Fee applicable for MGT-10 will be as per the Table of Fees prescribed in Companies (Registration Offices and Fees) Rules, 2014.

2. This issues with the approval of the Competent Authority.
